Our Mission

AviAgent.ai is building an AI-powered assistant to help pilots and maintenance crews quickly find information from technical manuals like AMM, IPC, Wiring Diagrams, and regulatory documents (FAA/EASA). Our goal is to simplify aircraft maintenance and improve operational efficiency.

Key Features

  • AI-Powered Search: Instantly retrieve procedures, diagrams, and checklists.
  • Human in loop:Monitored by Experienced engineers and technical experts.
  • Interactive Manual Assistance: Get step-by-step guidance from AMM, IPC, and SRM.
  • Smart Maintenance Logging: Log and track maintenance tasks for compliance.
  • FAA/EASA Regulation Lookup: Quickly access critical compliance guidelines.
  • Mobile & Tablet Optimized: Designed for technicians on the go.
